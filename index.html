About SriLankanization

Empowering Youth for Tomorrow's Tech. Closing educational gaps, instilling digital literacy nationwide.

Trainocate aligns with Sri Lanka's knowledge-based economy, focusing on long-term economic goals. We foster local talent in dynamic fields like data and AI, tapping into a workforce of 200,000. With the IT exports sector poised to reach $5 billion by 2025, Trainocate aims to empower Sri Lankan talents, supporting sustainable growth and positioning the country as a global tech player. As the first step to achieve, our plan is to train 10,000 individuals .
